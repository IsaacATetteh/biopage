Desmond Thomas Doss

The Man who refused to touch a gun

Here's a time line of Desmond Doss' life:

  • 1918: Desmond Doss is Born
  • 1939: World War 2 Begins
  • 1942: Doss was sent to Fort Jackson in South Carolina for training
  • 1944: Battle of Guam starts
  • 1945: 77th landed in Okinawa where Doss is considered a liability
  • 1945: Doss saved 75 men while not touching a firearm and under enemy fire
  • 1945: Doss was shot in his arm and stretched away
  • 1945: Doss was awarded the medal of honor for his bravery
  • 1976: Doss becomes completely deaf after and overdose of antibioticcs
  • 1991: His Wife Dorothy Doss is killed in a car crash
  • 1993: Doss remarries to a woman by the name of Fances May Dunman
  • 2006: Doss passes away

"I couldn't picture Christ with a rifle killing people." this quote from Desmond Doss showing his ambition to stand up for what he believed in. He spent his childhood with a fervent belief in the holy bible.
